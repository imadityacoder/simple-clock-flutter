# Privacy Policy for Analog Clock App

_Last updated: January 2026_

## Introduction

Analog Clock App respects your privacy. This application is designed to be simple and lightweight and does **not collect, store, or share any personal information**.

This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the app.

---

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The Analog Clock App:

- Does NOT require user accounts
- Does NOT collect names, emails, phone numbers, or locations
- Does NOT access contacts, camera, microphone, or storage
- Does NOT track users
- Does NOT run analytics or ads

---

## Local Storage

The app may store **non-personal preferences locally on your device**, such as:

- Selected clock style or theme

This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not shared with anyone
- Is used only to improve your app experience

---

## Third-Party Services

This app does **not use any third-party services** that collect user data.

---

## Internet Usage

The app works **fully offline** and does not send or receive any data over the internet.

---

## Children’s Privacy

This app is safe for all age groups.  
We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

---

## Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data leakage from the app.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be published in this repository.
